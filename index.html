Biography

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). My current research focuses on CKKS homomorphic encryption and privacy-preserving large-model inference based on homomorphic encryption.

During my undergraduate studies at NUDT, I worked on navigation satellite positioning, which built my foundation in practical system-oriented research.
